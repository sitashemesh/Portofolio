WEB DESIGN

Just In Case

The was one of the first projects I took part in, made to combine all of our newly learned abilities from the study. This means using design, HTML and CSS, to form a mobile first, responsive site that would sell phone covers.

This project rotated around understanding our audience while making a concept for selling phone covers. Today you can hardly avoid giving your child a phone in their hand, which they quickly destroy as soon as you turn your back. Therefore we chose to market our project to two target groups, children and parents, where we tried to give parents the opportunity to save money in the long run, and children the opportunity to keep their phones at hand in a stylish and colorful manner.

This project was about understanding ecommerce and SEO, and using wordpress along with its plugins to broaden our horizons in what and how we can create a website.
